Operation Sindoor

Named Operation Sindoor by Prime Minister Modi in poignant homage to the widows of the Pahalgam tragedy. The term “sindoor” (vermilion) represents marital sanctity—its symbolic spilling in the operation graphic conveyed both cultural grief and justice served.

  • Op. Vijay
  • Surgical Strike
  • Op. Sindoor
  • Us

Launched in the early hours of May 7, 2025, it involved coordinated efforts of the Army, Navy, and Air Force. Nine terror camps tied to Jaish-e-Mohammed, Lashkar-e-Taiba, and Hizbul Mujahideen across Pakistan and PoK were targeted and neutralized with surgical accuracy.

Symbolized a doctrinal shift from strategic restraint to targeted, proportional punitive action—blurring the lines between non‑state terror actors and the state that harbours them.

Claimed to have neutralized over 100 terrorists, destroyed terror infrastructure, and revealed vulnerabilities in Pakistani air defenses—while Indian assets remained unscathed.
