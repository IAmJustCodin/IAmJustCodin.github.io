Operation Sindoor(2025)

In February 2019, India's Air Force struck a terrorist camp in Balakot, Pakistan, in response to the Pulwama attack. This bold move targeted terrorist groups and increased tensions between the two countries.

The airstrike was a response to the Pulwama terrorist attack (Feb 14, 2019), where 40 Indian CRPF personnel.

On February 26, 2019, the Indian Air Force carried out a pre-emptive strike on a JeM terrorist training camp in Balakot, Pakistan, using Mirage 2000 jets and SPICE-2000 bombs.

India claimed it eliminated a large number of terrorists, while Pakistan denied casualties and downplayed the damage. The exact toll remains disputed internationally.
