Armypedia

Armypedia is a digital encyclopedia dedicated to the Indian Army. It brings together stories of bravery, historical operations, and the lives of soldiers, making the Army’s legacy accessible to everyone. Through Armypedia, users can learn about the Army’s achievements, traditions, and the sacrifices made by its personnel in service of the nation.
